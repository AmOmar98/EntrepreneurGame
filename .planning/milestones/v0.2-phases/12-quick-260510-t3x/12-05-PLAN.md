Convention nommage :
- snake_case Postgres `bonus_type` → string-literal union TS `BonusType`
- snake_case Postgres `multiplier_factor` → camelCase TS `multiplierFactor`
- snake_case Postgres `created_at` → camelCase TS `createdAt`
- nullable Postgres → `string | null` ou `number | null` TS
